About Volunteering

Volunteer ... Be The Change

Stemming from Tkiyet Um Ali’s belief in the importance of spreading awareness about food poverty, and with an aim to enhance social solidarity that plays an instrumental and influential part in achieving its goals, TKiyet Um Ali welcomes dedicated volunteers striving to help from all over the Kingdom.

Since 2014, Tkiyet Um Ali has welcomed more than 40,000 volunteers from various sectors and age groups who all contributed in serving their community and supporting Tkiyet Um Ali’s endorsed families, preserving their dignity, and helping them improve their living conditions.

Tkiyet Um Ali is one of the leading NGOs that emphasizes the importance of volunteering in Jordan and its impact on the community through its 16 volunteering programs.
